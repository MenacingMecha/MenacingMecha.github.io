Description

Y2ROLL is an N64-style minimalistic physics-based arcade-style marble rolling game that will take you back to the turn of the millennium.

Engine

Godot 3

Features

  • Minimalistic gameplay that is easy to pick up, with a very high skill ceiling and constant opportunities for skill expression
  • Easy to pick up - Every level simply asks you to reach the goal, and every level can be played with a single analogue stick or WASD keys
  • Hard to master - Later levels will test the movement mastery of even genre veterans
  • Play 100 levels across 8 worlds - either in Campaign mode, that stresses individual level mastery, or Arcade mode, stressing consistency in a life-based endurance challenge
  • Vibrant N64-styled graphics and art direction
  • Soundtrack inspired by atmospheric/intelligent DnB music
  • Create custom user levels for endless replayability with our FOSS level compiler
